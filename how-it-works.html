How WorkoutFinder Works

  1. Tell Us Your Goal: Choose what you want to achieve (e.g., build muscle, lose fat, improve strength, etc.).
  2. Select Target Muscles: Pick the muscle group(s) you want to focus on (e.g., glutes, chest, back, arms, etc.).
  3. Get Personalized Recommendations: Our smart algorithm suggests the best exercises for your goals and target muscles, with clear instructions and tips.
  4. Track & Progress: Use our app to keep track of your workouts and see your progress over time.
