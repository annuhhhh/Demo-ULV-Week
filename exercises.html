Hip Thrust

Muscle: Glutes

Goal: Build Muscle, Strength

One of the best exercises for glute development. Focus on squeezing at the top and keeping your core tight.

Push-Up

Muscle: Chest, Arms

Goal: Strength, Endurance

A classic bodyweight exercise for upper body strength. Keep your body in a straight line and lower with control.

Deadlift

Muscle: Back, Legs

Goal: Build Muscle, Strength

Targets the entire posterior chain. Maintain a flat back and drive through your heels.
